Three rules that keep technical posts readable:

1. **Lead with the conclusion.** Readers decide in the first paragraph
   whether to keep going. Give them the answer, then the reasoning.
2. **Show the code.** One real code block beats three paragraphs of prose
   describing what the code does.
3. **Keep it short.** A thousand words is usually 400 words of content and
   600 words of preamble. Cut the preamble.
